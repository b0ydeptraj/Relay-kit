# standalone-taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to place existing or future layer-4 standalones without collapsing them into the hub layer.

## web-and-experience
Examples or likely members:
- agent-browser
- frontend or UI flows
- threejs
- ui-heavy implementation
- next-product-frontend

## backend-and-data
Examples or likely members:
- backend-development
- databases
- API-heavy services
- jobs and queues
- go-service-engineering
- mmo-http-api-automation

## platform-and-infra
Examples or likely members:
- devops
- deployment
- CI/CD
- observability

## growth-and-localization
Examples or likely members:
- growth-marketing
- market-research
- mmo-social-marketing-automation
- mmo-reup-automation
- vietnamese-product-localization

## automation-and-operations

- automation-ops
- mmo-account-operations
- mmo-browser-fleet-automation
- mmo-lowcode-automation
- mmo-mobile-app-automation
- mmo-cloud-operations-automation
- runbooks
- schedulers
- webhooks

## commerce-and-integrations
Examples or likely members:
- shopify
- payments
- catalog or checkout flows

## media-and-content
Examples or likely members:
- media-tooling
- content pipelines
- asset-heavy workflows

## mmo-intelligence
Examples or likely members:
- mmo-onchain-security-audit
- mmo-llm-automation

Treat the taxonomy as a classification guide, not as a requirement to ship every standalone in one release.
